Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki is a spicy rice cake that you won't be able to resist once you try. Its chewy texture, with a touch of acnchovy stock and dried kelp, make for a rich flavor that will quickly become your favorite "Seoul" food.

Ingredients

Main

  • 350g Korean Rice Cakes
  • 150g Korean Fish Cakes, Rinsed and cut into bit sized pieces
  • 2 cups Korean Soup stock
  • 60g onion, thinly sliced

Tteokbokki Sauce (mix in a bowl)

  • 3 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp gochugaru

Garnish

  • 1 tsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame oil, lightly drizzled
  • 1 stalk green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Unless you have soft rice cakes, soak in warm water for 10 minutes
  2. Boil the soup stock in a shallow pot over medium heat and disolve the tteokbokki, stirring with a spatula. Once the stock is boiling, add the tteok (rice cakes), fish cakes, and onion. Boil for 3-5 mins until the rice is fully cooked. To thicken the sauce, simmer it on low heat for another 2-4 minutes.
  3. Add the sesame oil, sesame seeds, and the green onion. Give it a quick stir, serve, and enjoy!
